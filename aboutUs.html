About Us

Welcome to the Eagles Wings Portmore Mentorship Programme! This Program was launched in July 2022 and is geared
towards engaging and supporting the needs of youths between the ages of 12-20years living in Portmore, St.Catherine.

we are anticipating that through collaboration activities, youths in Portmore will receive guidance,
information and encouragement that will build their character and develop the skills for seeing positive life goals.

It is our hope that one day our mentees will one day further serve their community as role models, guiding others as they once were

Join our Mentorship Program today and embark on an enriching journey of growth and development.
